To the One I Let the Wind In

I had started to admire. Beneath the skies, I thought I had finally found a friend who is exactly like me.

I had started imagining convos, meetups. Why did I let my brain get drenched by your presence?

Now I feel empty. The call I had happily promised is just leaving behind an unmendable hole in me.

I am happy that you had the audacity to tell me. I am happy that you trusted.

But why do I feel like my heart is silently being betrayed in all the ventures it commits to?

I can't hold it in me anymore. I had allowed myself to let the wind in even when I knew it would destroy me.

Now why do I feel lonely again in this world full of people?

Why does it happen with everyone my heart chooses to?

My own expectations just hurt me.

But one thing I acknowledge, I am not in that intolerant pain anymore.

My life just loves to play jokes on me so that I can later think of it and laugh.

Wish I could hold a special position in you, but it doesn't exist anymore.

People are gonna love you because of your fame and you won't realize they are fake and you will leave me.

Later it will be too late to realize.
